# Solaris ✨

**Solaris** is a curated gifting brand built around one simple idea: gifts should feel personal.

Instead of generic gift boxes, Solaris creates themed hampers inspired by personalities, aesthetics, fandoms, and interests. Every hamper is designed to tell a story and help people gift something that feels thoughtful, memorable, and unique.

From Swifties and Batman fans to self-care lovers and soft-girl aesthetics, Solaris offers carefully curated hampers that make gifting effortless.

## Our Mission

We believe the best gifts are the ones that make someone say:

*"This is so me."*

Our goal is to transform gifting from a last-minute task into a meaningful experience by creating hampers that reflect personalities, passions, and moments.

## What We Offer

* Curated aesthetic hampers
* Personality-based gifting
* Custom packaging and presentation
* Ready-to-gift experiences
* Instagram-first ordering experience
